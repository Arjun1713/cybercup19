About Cyber Cup

CyberCup is the annual technical festival of Amity University, Noida curated on behalf of the Department of Computer Science of Amity School of Engineering and Technology.
It aims to appeal to the Tech enthusiasts in the form of a series of events enveloping various technological terrains with events such as BlendIt for strengthening the designing skills, HandsOn to make the participants aware of the latest widgets & gizmos and CodeIt, a platform to present their ideas and work along with many others.
Cyber Cup concurrently helps tech fanatics in exploring their capabilities and indulging in competitive contests accompanied by numerous fun elements perpetuating the upbeat spirit of a festival.

CodeIt

“Truth can only be found in one place: the code.”

A programmer's code is the symbol of his creative intuitions as well as their aptitude.
So why not allow your creativity to bloom and let your hard work pay off?

For all the budding developers out there, here's a chance for showcasing your self-created apps, games and websites to stand out in the crowd and thrive thoroughly into the fascinating world of code with CodeIT.

“CodeIT; the perfect platform for the budding developers”

Rules and Regulations :

1. Participants need to present an app, site or game that they have already prepared beforehand.
2. Maximum time for the presentation will be 5 minutes.
3. Unruly behaviour with coordinators, volunteers or other teams would not be tolerated and would result in direct disqualification.
4. The decision by the judges shall be considered as final and cannot be questioned.
5. Use of unfair means will lead to direct disqualification.

Judging Criteria :-

1. Ideas and Creativity
2. Coding Skills
3. Presentation of Ideas

Hackathon

All your life you’ve been thinking and this is your chance to bring all of them to life . Hackathon is one of our biggest and most promising event of CYBER CUP ’19 . It is an onsite competitive programming contest where the participants don’t just need to solve the problems but write their code using the minimum number of characters . The smartest solution wins it all !

Entries will be judged on the use of the full analytics process- incorporating not only the technical analysis and solution, but also the understanding of the business problem. It must hold clarity, technical strength of methodology and uniqueness of approach .

TIMELINE

The timeline will be segregated into different phases which are:
Phase 1: All participants will be provided with multiple themes. they will have to choose one out of the many themes and develop a prototype with the same.
Phase 2: Evaluation of the progress of the prototype of the participants
Phase 3: The participants will have to present the complete prototype for final evaluation.
Phase 4: Segregation of the teams into top 10.
Phase 5:Pitching by top 10 teams.

RULES

Team Size:
At max 4 members in a team. Lone wolves are welcome too, we might team you up during the hackathon.

Originality of idea :
idea should be unique and out of the box thinking which means already existing vs new solution

Final Decision:
The ultimate decision regarding awards and prizes lies within the hands of judges.

Equipment:
Participants have to bring their own laptops. Extension cords and internet access will be provided.

JUDGING CRITERIA

1. JUDGING CRITERIA IN THE SECOND PHASE WILL BE BASED UPON YOUR APPROACH TO THE PROBLEM , DESIGN AND CREATIVE SOLUTION
2. USEFULNESS AND USABILITY
3. RELEVANCE

BlendIt

Photoshop gives life to an images, colour to one's thoughts and platform for one's imagination.

All the modern day virtual world Picassos, adjust your LCD canvasses and get ready to splash colours away from your digital colour palette.

Feed the fire for the artistic genius in you and create your own digital masterpiece. BLENDIT brings to you the perfect platform to showcase your talent and test your Photoshop skills.,

BLENDIT will comprise of two rounds-

•Round 1
Participants will be given an image to work their magic on with full creative liberty.
•Round 2
Participants will be provided with multiple images and a choice to pick up any three to use together to form a single image with an even more creative caption suitable to the final image.

RULES :

1) Participants will be judged on the basis of these two rounds
2) Decisions will be final subject to judges result, with no provision for any kind of debate or argument.
3) Unruly behaviour with coordinators, volunteers or other team members will not be tolerated and will result in direct disqualification.

JUDGING CRITERIA :

1. Photoshop skills
2. Creativity
3. Technique
4. Originality

TechWizard

Do you reckon that you are at par with the analytics in your code!!!. Time to push your boundaries and stand out in the crowd with TechWiZ. The event comprises of challenges ranging from debugging to blind coding and will allow the participants to delve deep into the diminutive tracks of competitive coding. TechWiZ provides a perfect room for participants to assess their coding skills with the timers muddling with their brains for maintaining a competitive instinct.

There is a triad of rounds:

1. Debugging Round:
The participants have to catch the maximum number of bugs in a limited time frame.

2. Blind Coding:
The participants will have to blindly code a particular set of code blocks.

3. Technical Coding Round:
Coding challenges based on technical aptitude with varying difficulty levels will have to be decoded by the participants.

Rules and Regulations:

1). All the prerequisites such as PC's and writing material shall be provided at the event venue.
2). Any kind of unfriendly or malignant behaviour with volunteers, coordinators or fellow competitors will lead to disqualification.
3). The decision by the judges shall be binding and cannot be questioned.
4). Use of unfair means will result into immediate disqualification.

JUDGING CRITERIA :

1). Technical Proficiency
2). Debugging skills
3). Blind coding ability in a limited time constraint.
